# Localities (User)

Collection managers can review and update locality information within the CMS.

## Accessing Localities
1. Log in to the CMS.
2. Use the navigation menu to open **Localities**.

## Filtering and Reviewing
- Use the filter panel to search by abbreviation, name, or geological time abbreviations (M, Pi, Pe, H).
- The locality list shows geological time abbreviations joined with `/` and the number of linked accessions.
- Click **Print** to open a printable summary that lists localities in two columns with a geological time legend.
- Click a locality abbreviation to view details, including the geological time selection.

## Editing a Locality
- Select the edit icon on a locality to update the abbreviation, name, or geological time values.
- Save your changes. New localities must be added by an administrator.
